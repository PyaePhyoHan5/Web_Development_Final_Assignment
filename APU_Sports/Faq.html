What is APU Sports? +
APU Sports is a large-scale university sports carnival featuring competitive events across multiple disciplines.
Who can join APU Sports? +
All APU students, alumni, and invited participants can join the competition.
How do I register? +
You can register through the official registration page on this website.
Is registration free? +
Some events are free, while others may require a small participation fee.
Where can I view schedules? +
Schedules are available on the Schedule page with real-time updates.
Can I join multiple sports? +
Yes, participants can join multiple sports depending on schedule availability.
What happens if I miss my match? +
Missing a match may result in disqualification unless valid reasons are provided.
Are there team and individual events? +
Yes, APU Sports includes both team-based and individual competitions.
Will there be awards? +
Yes, winners will receive trophies, medals, and recognition.
Where is the event held? +
All events take place at Technology Park Malaysia.
